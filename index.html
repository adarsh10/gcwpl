About GCWPL
Golden Ceramic Works (P) Ltd. was established in the year 1984 with setting up it's manufacturing unit in Chirkunda, district Dhanbad, Jharkhand, India. In 1992, we started manufacturing Roof Set bricks for EAF and LRF, specially for foundry & casting industry @1500 MT per year

Today the plant has a total capacity of 10000 MT of Shaped refractory and 5000 MT of Unshaped refractory per year.

Our products are manufactured under closed surveillance by a team of experienced engineers and laboratory test as per the requirement of our consumer. We have a well equipped laboratory, which functions under the guidance of our director - Mr. Pawan Kumar Shah.

At present, our products are fired in state-of-the-art tunnel kiln, which is automatically controlled for better results. However some of the products which are also fired in traditional Down Draft Kiln for better result.
